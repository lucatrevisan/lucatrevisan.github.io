Fairness in Artificial Intelligence — June 27, 2022 @ Bocconi University

A mini-workshop on bias and discrimination in AI.

Talks will adress the legal framework around bias and discrimination; bias in natural language processing; bias in image processing; and bias in predictions.

The workshop will be open to the public.

More information and free registration to come in mid-April.

Organizer: Luca Trevisan (Bocconi)

Confirmed speakers:

  • Cynthia Dwork (Harvard)
  • Dirk Hovy (Bocconi)
  • Debora Nozza (Bocconi)
  • Symeon Papadopoulos (ITI)
  • Oreste Pollicino (Bocconi)
  • Omer Reingold (Stanford)
