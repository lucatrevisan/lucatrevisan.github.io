Fairness in Artificial Intelligence — June 27, 2022 @ Bocconi University

A mini-workshop on bias and discrimination in AI.

Location: Bocconi University, Roentgen Building, Room AS01

Talks will adress: the legal framework around bias and discrimination; bias in natural language processing; bias in image processing; and bias in predictions. The panel will feature the perspective of regulatory bodies, companies, and society.

The workshop is open to the public. Attendance is free but we require pre-registration:
